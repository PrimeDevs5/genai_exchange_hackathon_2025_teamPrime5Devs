**SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT AGREEMENT**

THIS SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT AGREEMENT (this "Agreement") is made and entered into as of December 1, 2024 (the "Effective Date"), by and between TECHCORP SOLUTIONS LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Company"), and INNOVATION LABS INC., a California corporation ("Developer").

**RECITALS**

WHEREAS, Company desires to engage Developer to develop certain software applications and related services as described herein; and

WHEREAS, Developer has the necessary skills, experience, and resources to perform the development services required by Company; and

WHEREAS, the parties wish to set forth the terms and conditions of their agreement regarding such development services.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

**1. SCOPE OF WORK**

1.1 Development Services. Developer shall provide software development services ("Services") to Company as described in Exhibit A attached hereto and incorporated herein by reference. The Services shall include:
   (a) Design and development of a web-based customer relationship management system
   (b) Integration with existing Company databases and third-party APIs
   (c) User interface design and user experience optimization
   (d) Quality assurance testing and debugging
   (e) Documentation and training materials

1.2 Specifications. The software to be developed shall conform to the technical specifications set forth in Exhibit B attached hereto.

1.3 Timeline. Developer shall complete the Services according to the timeline set forth in Exhibit C, with final delivery no later than June 30, 2025.

**2. COMPENSATION**

2.1 Development Fee. In consideration for the Services, Company shall pay Developer a total fee of $150,000 (the "Development Fee"), payable according to the following schedule:
   (a) $30,000 upon execution of this Agreement
   (b) $40,000 upon completion of Phase 1 (system architecture and database design)
   (c) $40,000 upon completion of Phase 2 (core functionality development)
   (d) $25,000 upon completion of Phase 3 (user interface and testing)
   (e) $15,000 upon final delivery and acceptance

2.2 Payment Terms. All payments shall be made within thirty (30) days after Company's receipt of Developer's invoice. Late payments shall incur interest at the rate of 1.5% per month.

2.3 Expenses. Company shall reimburse Developer for all reasonable, pre-approved, out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the Services, provided that such expenses are documented and submitted with appropriate receipts.

**EXHIBIT A - STATEMENT OF WORK**

**Project Overview:** Development of a comprehensive Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system with the following key features:

1. **User Management System**
   - Multi-role user authentication (Admin, Manager, Sales Rep, Customer Service)
   - User profile management and permissions
   - Single sign-on (SSO) integration capability

2. **Customer Database Management**
   - Contact information storage and management
   - Customer interaction history tracking
   - Lead scoring and qualification system
   - Customer segmentation tools

3. **Sales Pipeline Management**
   - Opportunity tracking and management
   - Sales forecasting and reporting
   - Quote and proposal generation
   - Integration with email marketing platforms

4. **Reporting and Analytics**
   - Real-time dashboard with key performance indicators
   - Custom report generation
   - Data export capabilities (CSV, PDF, Excel)
   - Advanced analytics and trend analysis

5. **Third-Party Integrations**
   - Email service providers (Gmail, Outlook)
   - Accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero)
   - Marketing automation platforms
   - Social media platforms for lead generation

**Technical Requirements:**
- Web-based application accessible via modern browsers
- Mobile-responsive design
- Cloud-hosted infrastructure with 99.9% uptime SLA
- GDPR and CCPA compliance for data protection
- API endpoints for future integrations
- Comprehensive user documentation and training materials

**Deliverables Timeline:**
- Phase 1: System architecture and database design (8 weeks)
- Phase 2: Core functionality development (12 weeks)
- Phase 3: User interface and testing (6 weeks)
- Phase 4: Deployment and training (2 weeks)
